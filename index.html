Strength Training Tips

Strength training involves using external resistance to challenge your muscles. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  1. Warm Up and Cool Down: Spend 5-10 minutes warming up (walking is great) and cooling down (stretching) before and after your workout.
  2. Focus on Form: Proper form is crucial. Align your body correctly and move smoothly through each exercise to avoid injuries.
  3. Work at the Right Tempo: Control your movements rather than relying on momentum. This maximizes strength gains.
  4. Pay Attention to Breathing: Breathe consistently during your workouts to maintain focus and energy.
  5. Gradually Increase Weight or Resistance: Challenge your muscles by progressively adding weight or resistance over time.
  6. Consistency: Aim for strength workouts 3 times per week. You can alternate between upper and lower body exercises.
  7. Teaming Up: Consider working out with a friend for motivation and accountability.
